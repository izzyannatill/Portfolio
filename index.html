At The McCulloch Workshop, we are committed to enhancing your spaces with the finest quality paints, including renowned brands such as Dulux, Farrow & Ball, Little Greene, Zoffany, Designers Guild, and Victory Colours.

These premium paints not only provide exceptional coverage and vibrant colors but also embody a commitment to quality and durability. In our pursuit of sustainability, we offer a selection of eco-friendly paints that prioritize both aesthetics and environmental responsibility, featuring natural ingredients and low VOC content for healthier indoor air quality. .

The paint sprayer is also an option for The McCulloch Workshop clients, providing a versatile solution for a wide range of painting needs. This advanced machine allows us to achieve a flawless finish on various surfaces, making it an ideal choice for projects such as kitchen cabinets, where precision and smoothness are paramount.

By utilizing a paint sprayer, we can apply an even coat that enhances the natural beauty of the cabinetry while ensuring durability and longevity.

At The McCulloch Workshop, we specialize in painting wardrobes to breathe new life into your furniture and enhance your living spaces. Our approach combines meticulous preparation with high-quality finishes.

We begin by carefully removing any hardware and thoroughly cleaning the surfaces, followed by sanding to create a smooth foundation for the paint. Utilizing premium paints, we achieve a beautiful, durable finish that resists wear and tear.
